I finished this work in the PUMCH, together with Dr. Fan Zhang from Beihang Univ. and other colleagues in 2026, supervised by Prof. Taiping Zhang and Prof. Wei Chen.

This study represents the math concept of  [stGrads](https://stgrads.readthedocs.io), a computational framework designed for both spot-size and bin-size spatial data to characterize the spatial gradients induced by specific cell types at both gene expression and cell composition levels. stGrads could identify the gradient-related genes for downstream work. We demonstrate the utility of stGrads on multiple spatial transcriptomic datasets, successfully identifying disease-associated spatial gradients. stGrads offers a generalizable and efficient tool for characterizing spatial interactions and functional responses in complex tissue environments with multi-omics data.
